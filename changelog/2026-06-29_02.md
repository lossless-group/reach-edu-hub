# Two more Strategy decks — and the collection comes alive

## Why Care?

A week ago the `strategies` collection was an empty menu waiting on its first
deck. It now holds **three**, and `/strategies` lists them as cards that each
open the full deck. The two added today both follow the same loop: an
LLM-assisted narrative authored in `context-v/narratives/strategies/<name>/`,
then designed into a Scroll-UI deck on the shared design system.

**Improving Literacy & Numeracy in Working Adults** is the one most directly tied
to Reach's mission outcomes. **NCAD Forge** is a curveball — an *operating*
proposal rather than a mobility case — showing the collection can hold different
shapes of strategy.

## What's New?

**Improving Literacy & Numeracy in Working Adults** (`/scroll/literacy-numeracy/ln-v1`)
— ten slides arguing that literacy and numeracy independently predict wages and
promotions, even controlling for education; that most working adults sit below
proficiency; that a degree can certify without lifting the basics; that the
pedagogy to fix this is finally ready (the science of reading reaching adult
education, plus contextualized numeracy); and that embedding it in the
Apprenticeship Degree *and measuring the gain* turns the degree into a capability
guarantee. Evidence from PIAAC / Hanushek et al., all figures flagged for
verification.

**NCAD Forge** (`/scroll/agentic-product-lab/forge-v1`) — ten slides making the
case that the Apprenticeship Degree runs on a different software stack than
campuses or online programs; that "vibe coding" moved prototyping from
engineering teams to domain experts; and that apprenticeship/adult-education
experts across NCAD's member network can now prototype that stack themselves,
with engineers hardening what proves out. Includes a voice-first angle for
assessment and evidence capture — speech-to-text and text-to-speech for teachers
and nurses who are on the go, mid-shift, with little time to write.

**Both** are built on `deck-primitives.css` (token-driven, three-mode) and use
grant-seeking / philanthropy language throughout — no investment framing; the
only "raise" is a worker's wage raise.

## What's Next?

More strategies are queued as draft narratives (e.g. Agent Workflow Maxxing —
agent workflows for travel, events, and the grant pipeline). Each becomes a deck
on the same system. Before any of these go in front of funders, the
`VERIFY`-flagged figures need confirming against primary sources.
