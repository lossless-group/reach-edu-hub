# A funder's voice on the strategy slide

## Why Care?

Lumina Foundation is one of the largest funders in adult-education and
workforce credentialing. When a deck arguing for **embedding** literacy and
numeracy into the Apprenticeship Degree can put Lumina's own framing on the
exact slide that makes that argument, the claim stops reading as Reach's
preference and starts reading as alignment with where major funding already
points.

## What changed

Slide 07 of the Improving Literacy & Numeracy deck (`/scroll/literacy-numeracy/ln-v1`,
the **Embed** slide) now carries a sourced pull-quote from Lumina's June 2026
piece *"A fragile foundation: The skills gaps holding adults back"* (Georgia
Reagan):

> "…integrating foundational literacy, numeracy and digital skills directly
> into workforce training rather than treating them as separate requirements."

The quote is rendered as an accent-bar `deck-card` `<figure>` with a clickable,
attributed source link — the deck's **first clickable-citation pattern**. Prior
sources in this deck are inline plain-text tags (`· AIR`, `· PIAAC 2023`); this
introduces a reusable, token-driven (`--color-accent`) form that tracks
light/dark/vibrant with no per-mode classes. Unlike the deck's draft figures,
it carries no `VERIFY` flag — it's a real, fetched quote with a live link.

Scroll-UI only; this deck has no Play-UI counterpart to mirror.
