# Five non-negotiable principles distinguish a Reach degree from any other online program

## Core messages

- **Efficiency** — 50% of learning happens through on-the-job work the candidate is already doing.
- **Flexibility** — Live seminars run evenings and weekends, designed around full-time work.
- **Relevance** — 100% job-embedded curriculum; theory connects to a practice the candidate performs that week.
- **Affordability** — Candidates are paid while studying and graduate with zero student debt.
- **Professional Capital** — Graduates advance inside the employer who sponsored them, deepening retention on both sides.
- This is the operating definition of "fully job-embedded" — and the standard NCAD is pushing other institutions to adopt.

## Verbatim quotes

> "Efficient, flexible, relevant, affordable, and grounded in conferring professional capital." — President Joe E. Ross

> "Reach brings higher education into the workplace and inspires deep learning through inquiry, dialogue, collaboration, and on-the-job practice."
