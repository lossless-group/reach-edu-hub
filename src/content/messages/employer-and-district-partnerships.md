# Hundreds of employers use Reach to grow specialized talent from inside their own workforce

## Core messages

- **550+ school partners across 10 states** (CA, AL, AR, GA, LA, CO, MS, TX, TN, WA).
- Operating across California, the Southeast, and a dedicated Behavioral Health region — each with its own VP of Impact.
- Partners "recruit and upskill existing employees for critical vacancies" instead of fighting over a shrinking external candidate pool.
- The partner ask is light: post materials, host info sessions, allow 15 hours/week of academic-classroom time, designate a liaison.
- Candidates apply learning to the workplace the same week they study it — productivity rises during the degree, not after.
- Reach is now extending the model to healthcare via the Apprenticeship College of Health, in partnership with the Training Fund.
