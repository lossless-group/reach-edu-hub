# Reach pioneered the Apprenticeship Degree — a fully accredited college degree where the workplace is the classroom

## Core messages

- Turn your job into a degree — roughly 50% of learning happens through on-the-job work, the rest through after-hours seminars.
- Job-embedded from day one through graduation: candidates never have to choose between a paycheck and a diploma.
- "A, B, C" framework: **A**ffordable, **B**ased in the workplace, **C**redit for the work you already do.
- These are real, fully accredited college degrees — not certificates, not alternative credentials.
- Reach launched the National Center for the Apprenticeship Degree (NCAD) in 2024 to drive nationwide adoption.
- The sector goal: **3 million Apprenticeship Degree completions by 2035.**
- The model is designed to extend across "every industry and community" — starting with teaching, now expanding to healthcare.
