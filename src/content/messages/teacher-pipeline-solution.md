# Reach is the proven answer to the teacher shortage — built from the staff already in the school

## Core messages

- Partner districts use Reach to convert classified staff into credentialed teachers without losing them to traditional college.
- Jefferson Parish Schools (LA) reduced teacher vacancies by half through the Reach pipeline.
- Named district partners include Jefferson Parish (LA), Russellville City Schools (AL), Pine Bluff (AR), Albertville City Schools (AL), and NorthWest Arkansas Community College.
- The Reach Teachers College runs the B.A. in Liberal Studies, B.A. in Global Education, M.A.T., M.Ed. in Instructional Leadership, and CA administrative credentials (PASC, CASC).
- Graduates of the B.A. in Global Education earn a Recommendation for Teacher Certification at completion.
- Coverage in CNN, USA Today, and ABC News positions the model as a national-scale shortage fix, not a regional pilot.
