# Five years in, Reach has a proven institutional shape — accredited, growing 20x, funded, and reaching 400+ communities

## Core messages

- Founded 2020 as a nonprofit accredited institution; reaffirmed by WSCUC in 2024.
- Undergraduate enrollment grew more than 20x since founding (900%+ in the first two years).
- **2,600+ candidates served and 400+ communities reached nationwide.**
- **$30 million in private and public grant commitments** secured.
- Coalition of nearly 50 higher-ed, state, employer, and philanthropic partners advancing the model.
- Founder Mallory Dwinal-Palisch (ex-Teach for America, co-founder Oxford Day Academy) remains as Senior Advisor; Joe E. Ross has been President & CEO since January 2021.
