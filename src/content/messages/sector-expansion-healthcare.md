# The Apprenticeship Degree is moving beyond teaching — Reach just launched a college for healthcare apprentices

## Core messages

- Apprenticeship College of Health launched in partnership with the Training Fund to address national healthcare workforce shortages.
- Featured by Inside Higher Ed ("A College for Health Care Apprentices") and Work Shift ("A Pioneer of Apprenticeship Degrees Steps Into Healthcare").
- Dr. Metoka Welch leads as Executive Dean of the Apprenticeship College of Health.
- The expansion validates the original thesis: the model is sector-agnostic, applicable to any workforce with credentialed roles and existing frontline staff.
- Behavioral health is the third operating region, alongside California and the Southeast — a separate VP of Impact runs it.
