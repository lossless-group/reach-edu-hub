# Skill and effort — not money or connections — should determine who gets ahead

## Core messages

- The mission is explicitly anti-gatekeeping: dismantle the financial and social barriers that decide who reaches a credential.
- The candidate base is workers already in the building — custodians, bus drivers, aides, paraprofessionals — long overlooked by traditional pathways.
- CNN, USA Today, and ABC News have all framed Reach as the model for turning frontline school staff into licensed teachers.
- Equity is operationalized: live seminars, 25:1 student-to-faculty ratio, on-site mentors, one-on-one advisors.
- Schusterman Family Philanthropies partners "arm-in-arm to train representative educators" in underserved communities.
- Apprenticeship degrees "create more opportunities for more Americans."

## Verbatim quotes

> "We envision a world where skill and effort, not money or connections, determine who gets ahead."

> "On-the-job learning and teaching make higher education accessible to all."
